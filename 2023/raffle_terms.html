ML4H Raffle

All ML4H participants will be eligible to earn tickets in a raffle on the basis of their participation in the symposium. Each participant will earn one ticket for their registration with additional tickets available by participating in poster sessions, round tables discussion, and exploring the Gather.Town space. Check out the Rocket Chat for additional raffle codes throughout the day! Prizes include Amazon gift cards and ML4H coffee cups.
