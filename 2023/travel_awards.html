Travel Awards

ML4H is delighted to offer travel awards for attendees of ML4H 2022 to New Orleans on Nov 28, 2022. We are offering up to 800 USD for international attendees, 500 USD for US-based attendees over a 250 mile radius of the ML4H venue, and 200 USD for anyone within 250 miles of the ML4H venue. In order to apply for a travel award please fill out this form in full by November 2, 2022. Note that travel awards for ML4H will only cover travel to and from the ML4H event venue (Intercontinental New Orleans), and a maximum of a one-night stay.

Travel awards will be announced by November 10, 2022. If you are a recipient of a travel award from ML4H, the reimbursement form can be found here. This form, along with clear scans/photos of original receipts should be emailed to info@ahli.cc by Dec 28th, 2022 for reimbursement. If approved, they should receive reimbursement by the end of Jan, 2023.
