Governing Board

The ML4H 2023 Symposium is hosted by the Association for Health Learning and Inference (AHLI), a not-for-profit organization dedicated to building an transdisciplinary machine learning and health community. AHLI works with its partners to advance health data quality and access, knowledge discovery, and meaningful use of complex health data. To learn more about the organization, visit www.ahli.cc.
